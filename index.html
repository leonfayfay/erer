About Ocean Guardian

We are a global non-profit organization dedicated to ocean conservation and marine life protection.

Our Mission

Ocean Guardian is committed to eliminating plastic pollution, protecting endangered marine species, restoring coral reefs, and creating sustainable ocean solutions for future generations.

Founded in 2012, we've grown into a global movement with volunteers and partners across 80+ coastal countries, working tirelessly to heal our blue planet.

We combine scientific research, community action, and policy advocacy to address the most critical threats facing our oceans today.
